Voltaki

Voltaki is a play on of the Portuguese language phrase "volta aqui", which means "come back here". With Voltaki, you can mark places to go back later using Google Maps™ app. It is just two clicks, one to mark and another to go back. The app can be used, for example, to find your car in a crowded shopping mall's parking lot, or in a street of a neighborhood or city you don't know well. It can be used also in group travels to mark a meeting point that everybody need to return to at the end of the day. And all of it with only one button!

The app also saves a history of the places you have marked and you can add a bookmark in the places you plan to go back some day, as for example that excellent restaurant you've discovered but that you probably will have forgotten the name or address after some months. You can also share your current location or a location from the bookmarks or history lists.
